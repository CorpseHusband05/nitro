The Legend of $NITRO

In the depths of a forgotten forest, a team of police officers came across an old, abandoned laboratory. a heavily secured door marked “Top Secret: Animal Testing.” Inside was Nitro, a slender and powerful being, the sole survivor of the laboratory experiments. Nitro awoke startled by her presence, her eyes glowing with intense energy.

In a flash she demonstrated her incredible speed and shot through the enclosure faster than the eye could follow her. When the police realized its potential, they freed Nitro and it escaped the lab. Outside, Nitro raced through the forest at high speed, surprising the police. From that day on, Nitro became a legend and was considered the fastest creature in the world
